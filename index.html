Kwonjoon Lee

I am a Computer Science Ph.D. student at UC San Diego, where I am advised by Zhuowen Tu. At Google I've worked on Lens Blur, HDR+, Jump, Portrait Mode, and Glass.

Before coming to UC San Diego, I received my BS in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University. I've spent time at Google[x], MIT CSAIL, Captricity, NASA Ames, Google NYC, the NYU MRL, Novartis, and Astrometry.net. I did my bachelors at the University of Toronto.

Research

I'm interested in computer vision, machine learning, statistics, optimization, image processing, virtual reality, and computational photography. Much of my research is about inferring the physical world (shape, depth, motion, paint, light, colors, etc) from images. I have also worked in astronomy and biology.
